Let's Chill Homemade Ice Cream Website

Overview

I collaborated with Let's Chill Homemade Ice Cream to design and develop their website through WordPress, ensuring that once the initial build was complete, the shop could independently manage and update their content. Working closely with the owners, I aligned the website's design with their vision, contributing to the color scheme and content while taking full responsibility for the site's layout, animations, and user experience. The website reflects the shop's playful and welcoming atmosphere while offering easy navigation for visitors. In 2023, Let's Chill was ranked #2 on Yelp's list of the Top Ice Cream Shops in the US and Canada, further highlighting their excellence and popularity.

Custom Design & Layout

I designed the website layout to provide an intuitive and engaging browsing experience. I strategically placed sections to highlight key areas like the shop's story, product offerings, and contact information. Custom animations were implemented to enhance the visual appeal and create a dynamic feel, ensuring the site remained both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

Interactive Menu System

One of the standout features of the website is the interactive ice cream menu. I developed a custom hover effect that displays detailed ingredient information for each flavor when users hover over a menu item. This feature not only enhances user engagement but also provides essential product information in a fun and accessible way, aligning with the shop's friendly brand identity.

Collaborative Development Process

Throughout the project, I maintained open communication with the shop owners, integrating their preferences for the color scheme and content while providing guidance on design decisions. By building the site on WordPress, I ensured they could easily update and maintain their website without needing extensive technical knowledge. My work provided them with a flexible and visually appealing platform to showcase their products and connect with customers.
